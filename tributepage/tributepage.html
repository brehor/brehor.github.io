Grumpy Cat: A Tribute to Tardar Sauce

Grumpy Cat at VidCon 2014

Tardar Sauce, better known by her on-screen moniker Grumpy Cat, became an internet star known across the world. She had syndicated merchandise including clothing, books, and even a mobile game.

  • 2012 - After building a social media following, Grumpy Cat enters the limelight via the Today Show on NBC.
  • 2013 - Grumpy Cat appears on multiple outlets including Good Morning America on ABC, CBS Evening News, and Anderson Live.
  • 2013 - Grumpy Cat becomes Friskies Official Spokescat.
  • 2014 - Grumpy Cat appears on WWE Raw, and probably wins.
  • 2019 - Grumpy Cat passes away in her home. Thousands mourne. She will be missed.
